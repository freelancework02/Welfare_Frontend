From Taloja's Lanes to University Gates: A QEWS Scholar's Journey

By QEWS Team

Published on August 18, 2025

In the bustling lanes of Taloja, where dreams are as numerous as the people, the path to higher education can often seem distant. For countless bright young minds, financial constraints can dim the light of their aspirations. This is the story of Fatima (name changed), a testament to how targeted support and unwavering belief can turn the tide.

Fatima, a resident of Taloja Phase 1, always excelled in her studies. Her passion for science was evident, but as she approached her final years of school, the mounting costs of fees, books, and competitive exam coaching became a heavy burden on her family. The dream of becoming an engineer felt like it was slipping away.

A Timely Intervention

It was then that her family learned about the educational programs at Quadri Educational Welfare Society. After a heartfelt application process, Fatima was selected for our student aid program, which provided her with a comprehensive scholarship.

More Than Just Financial Aid

Our support for Fatima went beyond just covering her expenses. We provided her with access to our network of mentors and counselors who offered guidance on her academic path. This holistic support system was crucial in building her confidence and helping her navigate the pressures of her studies.

"QEWS didn't just give me a scholarship; they gave me hope. They believed in me when I was starting to doubt myself. Knowing that my fees were taken care of allowed me to focus completely on my studies. It was a gift that changed everything for my family and me." - Fatima, QEWS Scholar

From Aspiration to Achievement

With the support of QEWS, Fatima excelled in her board exams and secured a remarkable score in the state entrance exams. Today, she is pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering at a prestigious university in Mumbai. She has not only opened the doors to a promising career for herself but has also become a beacon of hope and inspiration for other young girls in her community.

The Ripple Effect of One Educated Child

Fatima's story is a powerful reminder of our core belief: educating one child has the power to uplift an entire family and, by extension, the community. Each student we support is a seed of change, poised to grow and contribute to a stronger, more prosperous society.

There are many more students like Fatima in Navi Mumbai with dreams waiting to be realized. Join us in our mission. Your contribution can write the next chapter in another success story.
